Day 1 Nairobi-Masaai Mara

We will depart from Nairobi at 0700hrs and drive through the Eastern escarpment of the Great Rift Valley underlined by scenic agricultural highlands. Our driver guide will make a brief stop at the Great Rift Valley Viewpoint (Limuru) to give you a chance to enjoy a breathtaking view of Mount Longonot from an altitude of 2200m above sea level.


Day 2 Masaai Mara Full Day

After breakfast, you will spend the entire day exploring the reserveâ€™s remarkably high concentration of game, rich tree studded grassland and rolling hills.


Day 3 Masai Mara - Lake Nakuru

Today we will have an early morning game drives then depart Masai Mara and drive to Lake Nakuru where we will arrive in the evening. You will have lunch at Narok before proceeding to Flamingo Hill Tented Camp for dinner and overnight (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner & drinking water).


Day 4 Lake Nakuru - Lake Naivasha

After breakfast, we will have an extensive game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park. Here you may spot a variety of birdlife including some flamingoes as well as other birds of prey. You may also have the chance to spot the white and the rare black rhino.


Day 5 Lake Naivasha- Amboseli

After breakfast, depart Crater Lake Camp in Naivasha for Amboseli via Nairobi arriving in Amboseli in time for lunch. Evening game drives will follow. Lunch, dinner and overnight at AA Lodge Amboseli.


Day 6 Amboseli Full Day

After breakfast, we will start game drives in Amboseli N.P. armed with packed lunch to be served on top of observation hill where you have amazing views of the park and Mt. Kilimanjaro.


Day 7 Amboseli/-Tsavo West

After breakfast we will embark on a road trip via Chyulu Hills to Tsavo West National Park arriving late in time for lunch.


Day 8 Tsavo West-Nairobi

After breakfast we will have the last game drive to enjoy the wildlife for one last time before driving back to Nairobi.

Tour Details


This 8 days safari takes you to Nairobi City, Samburu National Reserve, Lake Nakuru National Park, Masai Mara National Reserve, Lake Naivasha and Amboseli National Park, touching all the main tourist attractions in Kenya. It starts with a day excursion in Nairobi where you will visit opharned elephant calves at DSWT, then visit Giraffe Center for a Rothschild’s giraffe kiss and then at Bomas of Kenya where you will enjoy Kenya traditional dances and our rich culture. You will then proceed with great adventure to Kenya’s finest parks that leaves travelers fully satisfied especially with all the inclusions, sightseeing and game viewing, this great safari is excellent value for money and a splendid source of refreshment.


Highlights

  • Interact with elephant calves
  • Kiss & feed Rothschild’s giraffe
  • Dance with locals at Bomas of Kenya
  • Visit almost all main wildlife parks in Kenya
  • Interact with several Kenyan communities & wildlife
  • Witness the 8th world wonder during wildebeest migration

Itinery

Day 1 NAIROBI EXCURSION

On your arrival at the airport you are met by our representative, drive to Swiss Lenana Mount Hotel and later pick up at 9-10am for David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust, where you will interact with orphaned baby elephants (11am12pm).


Day 2 NAIROBI- SAMBURU

Wake up early and take your breakfast by 7am, leave Nairobi and drive north to Samburu National Reserve to arrive at around 1400 hours in time for lunch at Samburu Sopa Lodge. After lunch relax and refresh then proceed for evening game drive in the park famous as the place for the Samburu semi-nomadic tribesmen, the Reticulated Giraffe, the Grevy’s Zebra, Gerenuk, Beisa Oryx, Somali Ostrich and a host of the big game including elephant and lion. Dinner and overnight at Samburu Sopa Lodge.


Day 3 FULL DAY IN SAMBURU

Wake up early and take a cup of coffee then proceed for an early game drive in this lush and region of North then return to the lodge for main breakfast and relax by swimming pool or at your own leisure till later in the evening for another game drive. The water of Ewaso Ny’iro River attracts a profusion of game. This area is known for the rare species of wildlife that are endemic and only found in Northern Kenya (Reticulated Giraffe, Grevy’s Zebra, Gerenuk, Beisa Oryx and Somali Ostrich ).


Day 4 SAMBURU- LAKE NAKURU

After breakfast depart Samburu close the equator as you drive southwards to Lake Nakuru National park. Drive through scenic Thomson falls, rift valley view point and Menengai crater to arrive in Flamingo Hill Camp in time for lunch.

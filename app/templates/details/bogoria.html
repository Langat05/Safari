Day 1 Nairobi - Lake Bogoria

Pick up from your hotel/airport upon arrival, depart Nairobi and drive to the Great Rift Valley view point where you will have a stopover for around 15 minutes. Here you will have a chance to take pictures of the beautiful sceneries of the Eastern Great Rift Valley. Proceed via Nakuru town and have another stopover at the Equator where you will have a chance to take pictures.


Day 2 Lake Bogoria - Lake Nakuru

After a delicious buffet breakfast depart Lake Bogoria for Lake Nakuru National Park where you will arrive at Flamingo Hill Camp in time for lunch. Later at around 4pm start your evening game drive across the Pink Lake often referred so due to its Great masses of Flamingos not forgetting the famous white Rhino and black rhino found in this park.


Day 3 Lake Nakuru - Masai Mara

After early morning breakfast leave Lake Nakuru with game drives enroute for Masai Mara arriving in time for lunch at Mara Sopa Lodge. Later at around 4pm you will start an evening game drive in the most famous park and home of safaris; Masai Mara, search of the Lions, Cheetahs, Elephants, Buffaloes and other members of the Big five among many other wildlife. Dinner and overnight at Mara Sopa Lodge.


Day 4 Full Day Game Drive in Masai Mara

Set off early for a morning game drive to witness the animal kingdom at its most active hour, on your way to the famous Mara River where thousands of wildebeest and Zebras cross from Serengeti to Masai mara each year.

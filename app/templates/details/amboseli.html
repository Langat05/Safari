Itinery

Day 1 Nairobi-Amboseli National Park

Pick up from your Nairobi Hotel/ residence at 0700hrs and take the 6 hour drive to Amboseli National Park arriving in time for a sumptuous buffet lunch. Lying below the most famous symbol of Africa, Mt Kilimanjaro, and best known for its great elephant herds, Amboseli National Park is one of the iconic wildlife reserves in East Africa. Following lunch and a rest, enjoy an afternoon game drive. Later drive back to your camp site.
Dinner and overnight at Kimana Amboseli Tented Camp.


Day 2 Amboseli – Tsavo West National park

Tsavo West and Tsavo East National Parks cover a massive 4% of Kenya’s total land area. Tsavo East is the larger of the two, both parks lies, equidistant between Nairobi and Mombasa.

After an early breakfast, depart for Tsavo West National Park. Mzima Springs, the chief marvel here is a favorite haunt of hippos and crocodiles. Picnic lunch then proceed for an evening game drive to see large herds of elephants, buffalo, common waterbuck, Zebra, eland, rhino and a wide variety of antelope. Dinner and overnight at Severin Safari Camp.


Day 3 Tsavo West – Tsavo East National park

We leave the camp soon after breakfast and proceed to Tsavo East National park. Game is viewed as we exit the park. This park was famed for its large herds of elephant covered in red dust and fine scenery. We visit the scenic Lugards Falls, Mudanda Rocks (attracts elephants in the drier season) and Aruba Dam as we view game if time permits. Dinner and overnight stay atSentrim Tsavo Camp.


Day 4 Tsavo East National park

Full day spent exploring the park. Morning and afternoon game drives are offered. Meals and overnight stay at Sentrim Tsavo Camp. The landscape in Tsavo East is flat and drier than the west, despite having one of Kenya’s largest rivers flowing through the middle. Much of the wildlife is concentrated on the Galana River, which has plentiful crocs and hippos. Likewise, Kudus, waterbucks and dik-diks are common along the river banks.


Day 5 Tsavo East –Mombasa

Early morning game drive. Soon after breakfast, we proceed to Mombasa arriving at around 1530hrs. The safari ends with drop off at your Mombasa Beach Hotel (Booked by yourself).

Itinery

Day 1 Nairobi - Mt. Kenya

Pick up from your hotel/residence at 7:00am, leave Nairobi and drive toward central Kenya to arrive at Naro Moru River Lodge around 9:30am. You will take your packed lunch and start the hiking right away.


Day 2 Mt. Kenya - Lake Nakuru

After breakfast drive down the escarpment via the floor of the Great Rift Valley and arrive at Lake Nakuru, Flamingo Hill Camp in time for lunch. After lunch start game drives in this park where you will be welcomed by the Columbus Monkeys which has become friendly to the travelers though sometimes they may become unfriendly when denied to access in your car.


Day 3 Lake Nakuru - Back To Nairobi

After breakfast start game viewing at Lake Nakuru National Park, where you will have a chance to visit a unique sanctuary catering for a sizable number of the endangered rhinoceros and the Rothschild giraffe. Water bucks as well as the predators such as the lion and leopard are common at the park. Also to be seen is a myriad different variety of bird life in and around the lake.
